Problem Statement:
Citizens often struggle to access immediate legal guidance during disputes or urgent situations, leading to uninformed decisions and escalation of conflicts. At the same time, legal information is complex, fragmented, and difficult for the general public to understand or access in a timely manner. There is a need for an intelligent legal assistance system that can act as a real-time digital advocate by analyzing live dispute scenarios between parties and helping individuals understand their rights, responsibilities, and possible legal actions. Alongside this, the system should function as an accessible legal knowledge layer that simplifies complex laws and provides updates on evolving legislation.


System:
- Input types: Text, Voice and Documents (Hin/Eng support)
- Evidence Processing Layer (Hindi + English)/ Optional: Summarises the evidence and stores in Vector STM to counter Duplicate Evidence. Also tell about the evidence which can't be processed.
- Category Classication: can be more than 1 (a threshold Value.)
- Query construction Layer: For RAG, Takes in USer query + Case Category + Evidence Summary + STM.
- Rag: Ouputs Retrived laws with confidence Score for Themis
- Themis: Takes in Contructed Query + Rag Retrival, Generates a pre final Response.
- Confidence Engine: Computes Legal Strength + legal Meter.
- *Response Genration (text): final response Generation + Peaceful Suggestions. 
- Translation
- Modes:
   - Fast (Default): Quick, Less Accurate, No questions asked
   - Pro: Slow, Accurate, Clarification Questions.


Features:
- Nearby legal help section: Redirection when confidence is low or complex queries.
- Legal Position Meter
